Networking


I am proficient in Level 2/3 Networking. I've used CISCO routers and switches to set up a small enterprise network.

I made a topology in Packet Tracer that simulates a small business enterprise. I used VLANs to separate the different departments on the switches and port security was implemented. The IP addresses were configured to be set dynamically on the routers, each department again separated into subnets with VLSM. Dynamic NAT and PAT was used to facilitate an Internet connection.


When I finished making this in Packet Tracer, I replicated it all on physical switches and routers. I connected the routers and switches together using Ethernet and serial cables. Then I connected to the device using a console cable and set their configurations using PuTTY.
