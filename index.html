About Me


I was born in Singapore, and moved here when I was a child. My parents came to Australia to make a better life for my sisters and I. This upbringing, being raised in two cultures, gave me a great appreciation of the diversity of Australia.

My choice of study at uni was Neuroscience, as it was an area that had potential to be have the next big breakthroughs. The Science Communication course encouraged a creativity and engagement between audience and author that drew me. I was always learning new things throughout the degree, satisfying my natural curiosity.

The IT industry is now beginning to be the place of innovation for the world. This means there are new technologies coming out from it every month. It's a very exciting time to be involved with IT.

I would like a role that enables me to stay current and knowledgeable with the innovations of the tech industry. Ideally, I would be learning more skills during as well as polishing my current proficiencies.
